About LaCleo Foods

Founded in 2010, LaCleo Foods has grown from a small local supplier to a leading B2B food distribution company serving businesses across the nation. Our mission is to provide high-quality, fresh food solutions that help companies nourish their teams and impress their clients.

Our Story

What began as a family-owned business with a single delivery truck has blossomed into a full-service food distribution network. We've maintained our commitment to quality and personal service while expanding our capabilities to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes.

Our Values

At LaCleo Foods, we believe in sustainable sourcing, exceptional quality, and reliable service. We partner with trusted local producers whenever possible and maintain rigorous quality control standards at every step of our supply chain.
